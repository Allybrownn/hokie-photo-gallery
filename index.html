Newspaper

Although the days of reading print news paper seem really distant Virginia Tech still has some available for its students all over campus. They are usually free and highlight different events all over town.

The Ride

There are always many buses all over Virginia Techs campus. They are affiliated with BT transit and they help students get all over campus and Blacksburg.

Lots of Hokies=Lots of trash

A dumpster near one of Virginia Techs dorms filled with trash.

Under the bridge

On a rainy day on campus under the Torgeson bridge is one of the only places students can stand outside to stay dry. The bridge also connects the library to academic classrooms.

Busy Students

Students doing homework in the Torgeson Bridge study area. There is ample space here for many students to work on whatever they need to.

Biker Mode

There are many students who cycle around Virginia Techs campus, this photo highlights a bike gear on a bike on the Virginia Tech campus.

liberal arts entrance

The liberal arts and human sciences building is one of the oldest buildings on campus. This highlights the unique entrance to the building.

Light

There are many lights all over the Virginia Tech campus which keep the campus illuminated at night. This picture shows one the the simple yet elegant light posts on campus.

Campus side walks are usually filled with students, scooters and bikes. This picture shows cars on the campus side walks making their way through.
